The purpose of Visualisation of DIT's Online Repository is to provide lecturers mainly in Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) an intuitive view of information such as publications, use of words and coauthorship about researchers in DIT who have published work in various disciplines. The information that is presented in the visualisations are real-time data that was scraped from DIT's Online Repository called Arrow. Different views of the co-authorship data consist of bar charts, network graph and bubble graph and for the publications of an author there is trend graph and a word cloud. The project was developed for a final year project by John Xaviery Lucente using AngularJS and Node.js. More details about the visualisation can be found in Help.
