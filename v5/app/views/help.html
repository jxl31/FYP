About Visualisation of DIT's Online Repository

The purpose of Visualisation of DIT's Online Repository is to provide lecturers mainly in Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) an intuitive view of information such as publications, use of words and coauthorship about researchers in DIT who have published work in various disciplines. The information are presented in visual graphics for a comprehensive overview of the analysed data. The data visualisation used are: Word Cloud, Bubble Chart, Network Graph, Trend Graph all of which are explained, along with various interactions a user can make with the graph. For a quick guide, go to Getting Started.
